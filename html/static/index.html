Register & Subscribe

Generate your key pair consisting of a public key (which will be registered with your account) and a private key (which does not leave your possession). 

$> ./colour init

When you create an Aletheia Ware account with a unique alias and a password of at least 12 characters, a 4096bit RSA public/private key pair is generated. Your alias and public key are registered with Aletheia Ware servers, and your private key is encrypted with your password and stored on your device - it does not leave your possession and therefore only you can decrypt your data - keep it safe!

To prevent loss of access to your data in case your device gets damaged, lost, or stolen, Aletheia Ware recommends provisioning multiple devices (see Export & Import below) and making a hard copy of your key pair and storing it in a secure location such as a fire-proof safe, or bank vault.

Export & Import

Your public/private key pair can be exported to other devices giving you easy access to your data from any supported device. Provisioning your key pair on multiple devices can help prevent loss of access to your data in case one of your devices gets damaged, lost, or stolen.

Sample Usage

Environment 

  • $> ALIAS=alice ./colour

    Sets the alias for Colour to load from the keystore.

Commands 

  • $> ./colour init

    Initializes environment, generates keys, and registers alias.

  • $> ./colour list

    Displays a list of canvases in reverse-chronological order.

  • $> ./colour show [hash]

    Displays the metadata of the canvas with the given hash.

  • $> ./colour showall [mode]

    Shows all canvases with the given mode.

  • $> ./colour customer

    Displays Stripe customer information.

  • $> ./colour subscription

    Displays Stripe subscription information.
